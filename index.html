phone_iphone

Web App

 2019
Trade gold

I admire the way this project was organized.

A friend approached me about their business.
They are wholesale gold traders who were relying on phones to interact with their customers. With approximately 250 customers, their phones were ringing around 800 times per day, but only about 200 of those calls led to actual deals, while the rest were just inquiries about prices.
My friend expressed “We never want to hear the phone ring again.”.



In less than three months, I developed this app with a plethora of options. Now, their phone hardly rings.
They have over 600 deals per day, and we've also successfully sold this app to four other wholesale gold traders.
This system now boasts over 1k users and facilitates over 2k daily deals."

My project

 2019
Smart menu

One evening, while dining with my friends at a restaurant, we all made our menu selections, and the waiter came to take our orders.

When I placed my order, the waiter apologized, saying, “Sorry, we don't have that item available right now”.
In that moment, all my friends, and even people at the nearby table, were waiting for me to make another choice. I didn't want to be in that situation, so I quickly selected another dish.

Reflecting on the experience, I realized I had done nothing wrong; it was the restaurant's problem. However, I felt they couldn't do much because their physical menu was on the table.

wb_incandescent   That's when I had an idea: what if the menu were smart! ! !



This was my first client

Over the next four months, I dedicated myself to this project, and despite finding it challenging to sell things, I managed to successfully introduce it to approximately 10 restaurants.
